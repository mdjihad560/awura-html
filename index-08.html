Quick answers for smart banking

It only takes a few minutes! Just sign up in the app, verify your identity, and you’re ready to bank.

Are there any hidden fees?

Nope! We believe in total transparency. What you see is what you get — no surprise charges.

Is my money safe?

Absolutely. Your deposits are FDIC insured up to the legal limits, and we use bank-grade encryption and biometric security to protect your account.

Can I send money to other banks?

Yes! You can transfer funds to any U.S. bank account easily, and transfers are often instant.

What if I lose my card?

No worries — just freeze it instantly in the app to block transactions, and order a replacement with a tap.

Do you offer customer support?

Definitely! Our team is available 24/7 via chat, phone, or email — always happy to help.
